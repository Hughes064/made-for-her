CLOTHING REVIEWS

Rapha Sweatshirt

Pros

The women’s 100% merino wool sweatshirt is the answer to staying warm without needing multiple layers. Seriously, this pullover will keep you warm. And cozy. It’s perfect for days when you wake up and just want to be comfortable, but you still need to head into the office. Its discreet pocket on the righthand size is perfect for holding small items, such as a house key or your credit card.

Cons:

After just a few washes, the vibrance starts to fade and the black begins to look more charcoal. It’s 100% merino wool, so if you’re at all sensitive to wool, this pullover isn’t for you. At $140.00, it ain’t cheap.

Conclusion: If you can snag this pullover on sale, do it.
