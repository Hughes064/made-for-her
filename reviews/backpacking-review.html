BACKPACKING REVIEWS

The Ghost Whisperer

Pros

I’m convinced that slipping into the Ghost Whisperer is what it would feel like to wrap myself around a cumulus cloud (science refresher: The cumulous cloud is often described as “puffy” or “fluffy”. But make no mistake, the Ghost Whisperer is not an overstuffed, marshmallow-style jacket). In other words, the Ghost Whisperer is a layer of warmth without any cumbersome weight. If the jacket didn’t keep me so warm, I might forget I was wearing it.

The Ghost Whisperer was engineered with extra space to accommodate layers beneath it for the coldest days. Elastic bindings in the cuffs keep out moisture and a toggle hem to easily adjust the base too the jacket, if wearing it snugly against your belly is your thing.

Cons:

The warmth, weight, and compactibility makes this jacket worth its price tag ($320.00). That said, the price tag is $320.00.

Purely subjective, but the glossy finish is a litle much for my taste. I would much prefer the jacket have a matte finish - be a little less flashy, if you know what I'm saying.
