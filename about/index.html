mission

made for her is a gathering place for women. For passionate women. For women who are tired of annoyinlg extracting relevant information from articles geared toward men. made for her is a place for inspiration. To inspire women to be bold, to take challenges, to express themselves, to be themselves.

made for her’s mission is simple: To create a digital space for women that easily expands into the physical world.

visionary

Jennifer Hughes | Founder

Jennifer started made for her because she kept landing on websites made for men. Not that there is anything wrong with men, but being a woman, it created a bit of a, well, conflict of interest.

As the founder of made for her, she is tasked with everything, from developing the site to filling it with content to reaching its audience. She holds a degree in journalism and currently works for a mobile innovation company, where she is Project Manager.

Jennifer rises before the sun each morning, excited to begin her day. She is passionate about technology, traveling,and the outdoors. She loves listening to podcasts (Reply All, This American Life, Ted Talks Radio Hour, Death, Sex & Money just to name a few), reading, and cycling. She's a coffee connoisseur; she doesn't like to shop; and she doesn’t own a television (or a Netflix subscription).
